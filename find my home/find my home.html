Buying a new home is an important milestone, so we want to make sure it’s the right one for you. At David Wilson Homes, we offer a wide range of properties to suit all types of homebuyers, lifestyles and budgets.

New homes for sale

Whether you’re a first-time buyer getting on the property ladder, an existing homeowner looking for an investment opportunity or a growing family needing more space – we are here to help. We have 2, 3, 4 and 5 bedroom homes designed to the highest standards, energy efficient and equipped with contemporary features. All our homes are elegantly furnished and provide flexible living space to accommodate everyone’s needs. If you dream of living by the coast, we offer a fantastic collection of properties in the South West of England, including around the spectacular Jurassic Coast in Dorset. More of a countryside lover? You can find a brilliant selection of homes in Wales and Yorkshire. And if you love the frenzy of a big city, you can browse our homes for sale in Liverpool and Bristol.

Offers

Buying a new home with David Wilson also means you can take advantage of our great offers. Need help getting on the property ladder? With the First Homes scheme, you could buy a new David Wilson home with a 30% discount on market value. If you’re an existing homeowner looking to buy a new house, our Movemaker or Part Exchange schemes could be ideal for you.
